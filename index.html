ABOUT US

India's First Madrasa for Blinds.

Madrasa-E-Imdadiya for the Blind was established in 2010 in order to provide the Islamic Education for the children and youngsters of our town. Later we got the idea of starting madrasa for Blind people, So in 2012 we started the Madrasa for Blind students. Our Madrasa is the “FIRST BLIND MADRASA IN INDIA” to provide the full fledged Islamic Education and School Education for Blind People. Click here to read more.

Madrasa for Blind & Normal Boys

Madrasa-E-Imdadiya offers all the courses from Nazirah to Aalimiyath in Braille method. All the Islamic Books are typed and printed in our Madrasa itself. More than 70 Islamic books are available in Braille of languages like Arabic, Tamil, Urdu, Roman English and English. Till now 5 blind students have became Hafiz-E-Quran. Click here to read more.

Madrasa for Blind & Normal Girls

Madrasa-E-Imdadiya also offers Islamic Education and school education to Blind and Normal Girls with Hostel facility. Till now 1 Blind Girl students have became Hafiz-E-Quran and more than 50 normal girl students have became Hafiz-E-Quran. Click here to read more.

School Education

Madrasa-E-Imdadiya also offers school education for the Blind and Normal Students along with Madrasa Education. More than 15 students are studying School and Colleges along with Madrasa. All the School books are provided in Braille. Click here to read more.

Makatib

Madrasa-E-Imdadiya also manages several makatib for childrens and youngsters of our town to study as a part time. Click here to read more.

TESTIMONIAL - HAZRATH MUFTI ABUL QASIM NOUMANI DB

Alhamdu Lillahi Rabbilaalameen

Aaj batareeq 9 zilhijjah 1437 mutabiq 13 aug 2016 barooz e saneechar janab moulana md osman sb melvisharam aur unke rufaqa ke chand blind talabah ke saat darul uloom deoband ke daftar e ihtimaam me kuch waqt guzarne ka moqa mila .moulana md osman sb aur unke rufaqah bshamool moulana malack md ibrahim sb aur unke aizza nabina hazraath ki tasleem ke liye qabile qadr aur qabile rashk mehnat karrahe hain .nabina oon ke maqsoos rasmul qat me noorani qaidah aasan nahu quran e paak ke paare aur urdu tamil zabanoon me fazaail e aamal ke nusqe dikhlaye .aur bacchoon se padh kar sunwaye.


Isse behad masarrat hui.dwa karta hun ke allah tala in hazraat ki masayi ku qubool farmaye aur moulana ke bayan ke mutabiq mulk me taqreeban 30 lackh nabina musalmaan hain allah tala in sab ke liye is tareeqa e taleem ku hidaayath aur tarbiyat ka zarya banayen. Is kaam ki takmeel par jo zarooraten hoon sab ka aafiyat ke saat intizaam farmaye.aameen.


Wassalaam abul qasim noumani,mohtamim darul uloom deoband.
